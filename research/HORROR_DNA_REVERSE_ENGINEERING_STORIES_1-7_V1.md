Ordinary person + forbidden place/ritual/desire + ignored warning + escalating signs + reveal + threat-recontextualizing twist + doom-or-loop ending + lingering aftertaste.

### 2. HIDDEN DNA
In this volume the real horror is **betrayal and moral debt**: the monster is often a *trusted intimate* (01,03) or a *bargain you signed* (05) — the supernatural just collects what the human flaw owes. 07 turns the lens inward: the haunted may be the dead themselves.

Stack 6 layers; lead with **possession/loss of autonomy** (06) or **betrayal by an intimate** (01,03), and back it with **dying unacknowledged** (04).

### 5. RETENTION DNA
