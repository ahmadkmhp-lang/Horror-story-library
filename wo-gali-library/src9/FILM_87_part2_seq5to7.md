SEQUENCE 6 — जो जा चुका था (The One Who Was Already Gone — TRUTH REVEAL, strongest horror) [11:00–13:30] [BGM: low brass hit -> ringing silence -> swarm-roar]

[VO Tone: shattering — reaching for a boy who is already at peace]

SCENE BREAKDOWN: But as Madhav tries to grasp Chhotu's hand, his hand passes right through. He cannot touch him. And then Chhotu, no longer frightened but at peace, turns to him: "Dadaji, I left long ago. On that day, and every day since. The one who can't move on isn't me — it's you."
