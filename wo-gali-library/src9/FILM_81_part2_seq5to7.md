SEQUENCE 6 — वही पुराना झूठ (The Same Old Lie — TRUTH REVEAL, strongest horror) [11:00–13:30] [BGM: low brass hit -> ringing silence -> rising heat-roar]

SCENE BREAKDOWN: Vikram tells Bansi the ledger is too old to matter; he'll burn it, reopen the furnace, and they'll both get rich. He reaches to throw the ledger into the nearest furnace — choosing the cover-up, exactly as the murderer once did.
