SEQUENCE 5 — गैली में आग (Fire in the Galley — False Relief) [9:30–11:00] [BGM: tentative rising strings -> silence drop]

SEQUENCE 6 — कहानी पढ़ने वाले को लिखती है (The Story Writes Its Reader — TRUTH REVEAL, strongest horror) [11:00–13:30] [BGM: low brass hit -> ringing silence -> deep press-roar]

[VO Tone: shattering — reality itself coming apart in print]

उस अनजले पन्ने पर लिखा था: "खबरनवीस ने सोचा कि कहानी जलाकर वो बच गया। पर उसे पता ही नहीं था — ये प्रेस कहानी नहीं छापता। ये पढ़ने वाले को, दोबारा लिखता है।" और सच, बिजली की तरह मुझ पर गिरा। मैं कोई पढ़ने वाला नहीं था। मैं, इस प्रेस की, छपती हुई कहानी था।

SCENE BREAKDOWN: On that unburned page was written: "The editor thought he had escaped by burning the story. But he never knew — this press does not print a story. It rewrites its reader." And the truth fell on him like lightning. He was not a reader. He was the press's story, being printed.

SCENE BREAKDOWN: And then it all became clear. Master-ji's shade stood beside him. Master-ji: "By printing lies, you changed the stories of innocents, erased their lives. This press does only that — it changes stories. You taught it to lie, babu. Now it writes your story, out of your own lies. And what is printed, comes true."
