SEQUENCE 6 — याद ही झूठी थी (The Memory Itself Was False — TRUTH REVEAL, strongest horror) [11:00–13:30] [BGM: low brass hit -> ringing silence -> deep glass-roar]

[VO Tone: shattering — the self he believed in coming apart]

दरवाज़े के शीशे से, अमर का अक्स, धीरे से बाहर निकला, और मेरे सामने आ खड़ा हुआ — ज़िंदा, असली। और सच, आईने की तरह, मुझ पर टूट पड़ा। अमर: "तुम्हारी कोई याद सच नहीं थी, समर। तुम मेरे जुड़वाँ थे, पर हुनर मुझमें था, मोहब्बत मुझे मिली, ज़िंदगी मेरी थी। तुमने, जलन में, मुझे इसी कारख़ाने में मिटा दिया, मेरा नाम-काम सब ले लिया।"

SCENE BREAKDOWN: From the door glass, Amar's reflection stepped slowly out and stood before him — alive, real. And the truth broke over him like a mirror. Amar: "None of your memories were true, Samar. You were my twin, but the gift was mine, the love was mine, the life was mine. In envy, you erased me in this very workshop, and took my name and my work, all of it."

SCENE BREAKDOWN: And then it all became clear. After erasing Amar, Samar could not bear the guilt of it. So he gave himself a false memory — that he was the craftsman, that this life had always been his. For years he lived inside that lie. But these mirrors don't show lies. And they had returned to him his real identity — that of a thief, of one who erases.
