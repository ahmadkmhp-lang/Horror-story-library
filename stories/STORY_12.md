STORY 12
Title: Purani Haveli (Kaal Trighori)

SECTION 01: One-Paragraph Story Summary (as supplied — "Summary")

Seeking an escape from city life, a writer couple, Rehan and Neha, move into a cheap, beautiful, but abandoned heritage mansion (haveli) in a Maharashtrian village. Rehan soon discovers a hidden, locked trunk in the basement containing a cursed black doll wrapped in red cloth and a torn journal warning about "Trighori." Ignoring the warning, Rehan uncovers the doll, unleashing an ancient, cyclical demonic force known as "Kaal Trighori" that awakens every 100 years. As supernatural occurrences escalate—ranging from shattered lights to violently killed cats and a towering 10-foot shadow—Rehan enlists a local occultist (Pandit) for a dark ritual. The ritual goes catastrophically wrong when a terrified Neha burns the doll with her bare hands, violating a strict taboo. This act dooms the family, leading to the brutal deaths of Neha's parents, the occultist, and ultimately the couple themselves, driven to madness and murder-suicide by the demonic entity, leaving behind only bloody warnings.
