STORY 15
Title: Jantar Mantar Horror Story | A Dark Secret

SECTION 01: One-Paragraph Story Summary (as supplied — "Summary")

Rahul, a struggling gig-economy delivery driver, becomes infatuated with Shruti, a beautiful and wealthy girl he drops off late one night. Desperate for a shortcut to romance, he replies to a mysterious flyer promising to fulfill any desire via dark magic (Vashikaran) and texts them Shruti's photo, completely ignoring the warning that he must pay ₹5,000 once his wish comes true. The dark magic works too well, embedding a demonic entity into Shruti that makes her obsessively infatuated with Rahul to the point of violent, suicidal madness. When Rahul ghosts her because he finds her obsessive behavior "crazy," Shruti's condition rapidly deteriorates into full demonic possession, forcing her terrified parents to hire an exorcist. The exorcist captures Rahul, exposes his use of black magic, and spends months purging the demon from Shruti, leaving Rahul with immense guilt and Shruti with lingering subconscious psychological scars.
