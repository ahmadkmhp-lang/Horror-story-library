STORY 11
Title: Ouija Board - Conjuring Real Case

SECTION 01: One-Paragraph Story Summary (as supplied — "Summary")

The Donovan family’s relocation to a seemingly normal suburban home spirals into a demonic infestation when their isolated, strictly raised 19-year-old daughter, Patty, unwittingly invites a dark entity into their lives via a Ouija board. Believing she is communicating with the spirit of a deceased teenage boy who claims to be her "boyfriend," Patty extends an invitation for him to physically manifest, which triggers violent poltergeist activities, foul odors, and blood-like liquids pouring from the faucets. As the supernatural attacks escalate and follow the family even when they attempt to flee to a hotel, renowned paranormal investigators Ed and Lorraine Warren are called in, uncovering that the entity is a deceptive, ancient demon. A terrifying Catholic exorcism ensues, culminating in Patty's brief physical possession before the demonic force is ultimately banished, leaving behind a chilling reminder of the dangers of opening unknown supernatural doors.
