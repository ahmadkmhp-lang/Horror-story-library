STORY 13
Title: Haunted House: The Full Tale of Terror

SECTION 01: One-Paragraph Story Summary (as supplied — "Summary")

After moving into a new rental home with a direct view of a creepy, abandoned house next door, a teenage girl named Annie becomes obsessed with a mysterious figure she keeps seeing in its top-floor window. Ignoring her mother's dismissals that it's just a prank or a hallucination, Annie sneaks into the abandoned house at night to confront the prankster, only to become trapped inside while a shape-shifting witch steals her identity. The witch, masquerading as Annie, infiltrates her home and spends her nights brutally murdering and eating the locals. Annie’s mother grows increasingly suspicious of "Annie's" strange, withdrawn behavior and a disturbing nightmare leads her to discover the imposter drenched in blood in the backyard. Using a clever lie about a childhood doll to confirm the imposter's true identity, the mother traps the witch inside their car, burns it alive, and rescues the real Annie from the abandoned house, narrowly saving her life before they flee the cursed neighborhood.
